# gol-cam — Value Proposition

## What
A mini camera system that attaches to a button soccer goal (15cm x 4cm) and automatically detects when a small ball (4mm or 6mm) crosses the goal line, triggering a goal scoring event.

## Problem
Button soccer ("futebol de botão") players have no automated way to detect and celebrate goals. Disputed goals and missed calls reduce the fun.

## Solution
A small, attachable camera module with computer vision that:
- Monitors the goal area in real-time
- Detects when the ball enters the goal
- Triggers a goal event (sound, light, score update)

## Target Users
- Button soccer enthusiasts
- Tabletop sports players
- Makers/hobbyists

## Key Constraints
- Goal dimensions: ~15cm wide x 4cm tall
- Ball size: 4mm or 6mm diameter
- Camera must be small enough to mount on/near the goal
- Detection must be fast (real-time or near real-time)
- Low cost, portable, self-contained
