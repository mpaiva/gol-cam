## Key Findings
- Dadinho was officially recognized by CBFM in 2010 as the newest rule variant
- Rio de Janeiro originated the "Regra Carioca" (3 Toques) and has nearly 50 years of state championship history
- The dadinho is a small cube (~6mm per face, 0.1-0.3g) used as the ball
- Rules: 3-touch individual limit, 9-touch team limit, mandatory shot by 9th touch
- Active competitive scene with 16+ years of national championships
